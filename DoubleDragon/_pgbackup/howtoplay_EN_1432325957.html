How To Play - double vision

Color Clusters

Your goal is to make big color clusters with three or more blocks of the same color touching.

Co-op

Try it with mouse and WASD - or two gamepads. Shared clusters earn bonus points!

X2, X3, and X4

Collect Multipliers inside corkscrew loops at big song moments. Try to have a lot of color cluster matches for big points!


WHITE BLOCKS: You'll also notice special white blocks on the track. They never match in clusters, but do collect for big points if they touch the bottom of the puzzle grid.

X2,X3, and X4: The most important powerups are multipliers (x2,x3,and x4). You'll find them inside corkscrew loops during the song's biggest moments. They're also marked as white lines on the minimap. Hit multipliers to immediately collect all your matched color clusters with bonus points. Unmatched puzzle blocks are also cleared, for no points. Then bonus white blocks will be thrown onto your grid. The more matched color clusters that collected with the multiplier, the more bonus white blocks you'll get.

PAINTS and STORMS: These powerups are marked on the minimmap with color lines. At each one, you can choose to collect the storm or the paint. Paint changes all the blocks in your grid to a single color and Storm adds up to 9 new blocks to your grid (if there's room).

SCORING: Larger matches are worth more points. There's also a "rainbow" bonus for having different colors of clusters collect together. RED > YELLOW > BLUE > PURPLE. Hot colors are worth more points than cool colors.

CHAIN BONUS: Keeep hitting blocks (but don't overfill) to keep your chain multiplier high. It's shown under your score.

SOLO CHALLENGE: You can play double vision by yourself using both sticks of a gamepad.

CLEAN FINISH BONUS: End the song with an empty grid for a big bonus.

SEEING RED / BUTTER NINJA: Collect nearly all the red blocks and/or nearly all the yellow blocks for these end-ride bonuses.

GAMEPAD: Use bumpers, triggers, or the right stick for pushing.
